2. Connective Tissue Layers

  • Epimysium – surrounds entire muscle
  • Perimysium – surrounds fascicles
  • Endomysium – surrounds individual muscle fibers

3. Sarcomere Anatomy

Identify the Parts:

  • Z Disc – holds thin filaments
  • M Line – holds thick filaments
  • A Band – dark stripe (thick + thin)
  • I Band – light stripe (thin only)
  • Titin – elastic stabilizing protein
